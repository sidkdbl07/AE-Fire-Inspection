  5. Specifice ancillary devices indicated in the remarks column.

  8. Smoke detector cleaning or replacement date should be record in the remarks column.

  13. Upper and lower pressure setting of supervisory devices should be recored in the remarks column.
  14. Low temperature setting should be recored in the remarks column.

  16. Identify date field devices changed in the remarks column.
  17. Identify correct field device operation (e.g., alarm, trouble, supervisory, annunciation indication).

  23. Confirm field device free of foreign substance.
